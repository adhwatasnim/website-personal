Quarantine Routine

In the early days of the coronavirus pandemic, when it was just beginning to become clear that people who could stay at home would be doing so for a very long while, I was lazying around, having the time of my life as everything were stopped at place; the work, the university assignments, the class and the pressure. It seems that what I've been asking for my whole life has finally happened; to get a time off and to just do nothing at all. Everything was at peace of mind with no one to bother me at all, well, atleast except for my family members. I was being unproductive at my best, by sleeping late and waking up at noon as I was mocked by my mom for being the princess I am. Being in an asian family, that definitely would not impress anyone around and yet, I do not care at all.

However, things changed when I finally realized the things that happened throughout the world is indeed serious, and it is not going to stay for short time. This quarantine is realer than I had expected. I started to get anxious and hence, I am currently trying to make a change on my depressing routine as I need to do something just to feel good about myself. The sleeping schedule stays the same, but the day routine is what I just have to change. For all of the time that I've been overthinking alone in my small room, I had missed on what I used to enjoy doing. So, I started my day by working out first thing in the morning as it is the best time to work out with an empty stomach. Combined with the laziness that I still have, the workout would be strictly a good 10 minutes, as well as doing it at night time, precisely at midnight since I'm a night owl nowadays.
